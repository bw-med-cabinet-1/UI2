Our Mission

Our mission at Med Cabinet is to provide assitance to new users, age 45 and up, who are trying to get off pharmaceuticals, under Medical Professional reccomendation, and want to use cannabis as a means to battle medical conditions and ailments. By Proving our users with the ability to search for strains by ailment or symptoms, find a local dispensery, reccomended doseage, intake method, create dosing schedule, and keep a list of all reccomended strains and dispenseries we hope to promote effective usage.
